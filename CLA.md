## Why a CLA is Required

Signet Forge is dual-licensed under AGPL-3.0-or-later (open source) and a
Commercial License Exception (for proprietary use). To maintain the legal
right to offer both licenses, Signet Stack must hold sufficient rights over
all contributions. Without a CLA, a contributor retains copyright over their
contribution, which could prevent Signet Stack from including it in commercial
distributions.

This CLA is modelled on the Apache Individual Contributor License Agreement
and the Harmony CLA (Project Harmony, harmonyagreements.org).
